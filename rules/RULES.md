# Discord Rules Templates
This file contains ready-to-use rule templates for Discord communities. Simply copy the template you need and customize it for your server.

## Community
Copy and paste the following template into your server's rules channel:

```
# Welcome to {SERVER_NAME}!

We're thrilled to have you here! Before diving in, please take a moment to review our community guidelines.


## 📜 Discord Policy
- Respect Discord's **Terms of Service** and **Community Guidelines**  
  [Terms of Service](https://discord.com/terms) | [Community Guidelines](https://discord.com/guidelines)


## 🧭 Moderation & Behavior
- **Treat everyone with respect**  
  Harassment, discrimination, bullying, or threats will not be tolerated.

- **Follow moderator instructions**  
  If a moderator asks you to stop or adjust something, please comply.

- **Avoid mini-modding**  
  Report issues instead of trying to enforce the rules yourself — that's our job!


## 💬 Language & Conduct
- **Keep conversations sensible**  
  No drama, hate speech, NSFW content, or inappropriate topics.

- **English is the primary language**  
  You may include translations alongside English messages if needed.


## 📣 Promotion & Activity
- **No self-promotion or advertising**  
  Don’t promote your content, servers, or DMs without permission.

- **Keep the vibes clean**  
  Avoid spam, chain messages, or disruptive behavior that derails the chat.


## ❤️ Safety & Privacy
- **Protect your privacy**  
  Don’t share your personal info, address, or face. Keep yourself safe online.

- **No venting**  
  We’re not equipped for crisis help. Please reach out to professionals or hotlines.

- **Report mod abuse directly to the owner**  
  DM the owner privately for fast resolution — skip tickets for this.


## 🚫 Dating & Boundaries
- **This is not a dating server**    
  Take romance to DMs privately and respectfully.


## 🪶 Final Note
Rules may be updated over time — stay informed!  
Breaking them may result in warnings, mutes, kicks, or bans, depending on severity.
@everyone
```

## NSFW Channel
Copy and paste the following template into your server's NSFW rules channel:

```
# 🔞 NSFW Channel Rules

Welcome to the NSFW section. By accessing this channel, you confirm that you meet Discord's age requirements and are comfortable viewing mature content.

## 🚫 Prohibited Content

❌ No gore, mutilation, or graphic violence  
❌ No real-life death, injuries, or crime scene content  
❌ No content involving minors or characters portrayed as minors  
❌ No bestiality or animal-related sexual content  
❌ No incest content  
❌ No non-consensual, coercive, or assault-related content  
❌ No leaked, stolen, or private content  
❌ No revenge porn or content shared without permission  
❌ No illegal content of any kind

## ✅ Allowed Content

✔️ Adult NSFW content involving consenting adults  
✔️ Suggestive artwork, animations, and mature memes  
✔️ Adult-themed discussions (keep it respectful)

## 💬 Behavior Rules

• Respect everyone's boundaries and comfort levels  
• No harassment, bullying, or pressuring others into sharing content  
• Do not DM users unsolicited NSFW content  
• Keep all NSFW content within designated NSFW channels  
• Follow Discord's Terms of Service at all times

## ⚠️ Staff Discretion

Staff may remove any content they believe is inappropriate, disturbing, or harmful, even if not specifically listed above.

Breaking these rules may result in content removal, warnings, mutes, or bans.
```

Always ensure your server complies with Discord's Terms of Service and Community Guidelines.
